This whole process is really exciting to me. There is something extraordinary about being able to push my own experience. It seems like a chance to be responsible for my own learning experience.

I excited to see how it plays out in our cohort classrooms. I know when I take classes there is a part of me that is comfortable letting the teacher control the learning process. In the scenario I am just there to receive. What is being presented to me at Dev Bootcamp is an opportunity to engage my whole self in the learning process - not just recieving, but actively engaging, offering, and, questioning.

I don't think my expectations have changed. I knew, from a good friend of mine, what I was getting into before hand. Even so - if anything, I think I feel a little more nervous...
